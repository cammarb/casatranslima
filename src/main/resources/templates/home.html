Did you know that the main problem transgender people face is homelesness?

Learn more

Trans people face homelesness because of the following reasons


  • They get kicked out of their homes
  • They run away because of abusive circumstances
  • They do not have access to work, therefore they cannot afford a home
  • They face abuse and instances of transphobia at the hands of their tenants

Only 40% of Peruvians would hire a trans person.


According to Universidad Peruana Cayetano Heredia, only 3% of this community can access a formal job. Due to the pandemic, their situation has become even more precarious.
